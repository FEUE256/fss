If you buy the USB stick:

  1. Plug in the USB stick in to your computer
  2. Make sure that the USB stick is device E:\ (If it don't go to If you alredy have the E:\ drive on this page)
  3. Go in to the USB drive and click om "FSS_Run"
  4. Do what the program tell you to do and you is in FFCP

If you alredy have the E:\ drive

  1. Download and Extract the zip file from the Download page
  2. Copy over the files to the E:\ drive (Your drive most be as minimal 100 GB)
  3. Go in to the E:\ drive and click on FSS_Run
  4. Do what the program tell you to do and you is in FFCP
